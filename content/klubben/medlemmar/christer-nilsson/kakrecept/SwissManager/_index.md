### Installation av SwissManager

* [SM Download](https://swiss-manager.at/download.aspx?lan=0)
* Starta SM
* Other | Install
* [Display conditions for use]
* [Accept conditions for use]
* Skriv in licensen, 16 tecken
* [OK]

### Hämtning av FIDE:s ratingfil

* [FIDE Download](https://ratings.fide.com/download_lists.phtml)
* Rating Lists | Import rating lists
* [Import rating list]
  * FIDE
  * Ange filen
  * [Start import]
* [OK]

### Skapande av turnering

* File | New tournament
* [Swiss System]
* Tournament name: KM
* Time Control: 90+30
* Federation: SWE
* Rounds: 10
* Time Control: [Standard]
* Rated FIDE: [No]
* Rated National: [No]
* [OK]

### Hämtning av deltagare

* Input | Enter players
* [Input from List]
* Rating List: FIDE
* Int. rating from 1400 to 2500
* Date of birth: 1901 to 1960
* Federation: SWE
* [Start search]
* (nu hittades 704 spelare)

### Urval av deltagare

* Name or Code: Nilsson
* Dubbelklick
* [check presence of players]
* Name or Code: Flardh
* När alla hittats: [OK]

### Lägg till rating för båda spelarna

* [Print menu]
* [Define new list]
* Gå ner till 10 Rtg.Int. show. 
* [y]
* Gå ner till 28 Rtg.Int. show. 
* [y]
* [X End]
* Stäng fönster (5)
* [F10]
* (nu ska rating visas för båda spelarna)

### Mata in resultat [F7]

* Input | Enter results
* **Key** = **Button**
* [0] = Empty
* [1] = 1:0
* [2] = remi
* [3] = 0:1
* [4] = 1F:0F
* [5] = 0F:1F
* [6] = 0F:0F
* [7] = Adjourn
* (övriga via musklick)
* [End]
